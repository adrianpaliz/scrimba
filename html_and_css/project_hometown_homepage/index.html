Visit Guanujo

Enjoy beautiful landscapes, food, drink and culture in this small town.

Top three activities to do at Guanujo

Taste the famous Pajaro azul

You can try this famous alcoholic drink that come directly from the subtropical part of Bolívar.

Visit the beautiful landscape from La loma dulce

The spot called Loma dulce has a beautiful view that you can enjoy.

Take a trip to La Mailona river

This river is very cold but it is a wonderful experience take a walk and visit it.
